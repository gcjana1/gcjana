Short Biography

Mr. Gopal Chandra Jana is a Senior PhD Scholar in the Department of Information Technology, Indian Institute of Information Technology Allahabad under the Supervision of Prof. Anupam Agrawal. He completed Bachelor of Engineering (B.E) in Information Technology from the Department of Information Technology,University Institute of Technology,The University of Burdwan in 2015, and Master of Technology (M.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering with specialization in Computer Science and Engineering from the School of Computer Engineering, KIIT Deemed to be University in 2017. He has published 6 journal papers (SCIE and Scopus), 7 conference papers and hold one Copyright on a Software Tool. He is a Member of IEEE (USA), Student member of ACM(USA) and Associate Member of IE India (IEI). He is potential reviewer of several international journals.

Research Interests

EEG Signal Analysis, Biomedical Signal and Image Processing, Human and Brain Computer Interface, Vision and Signal Processing, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Explainable Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Healthcare, Portable Real-time product for Healthcare, Assistive technology solution for healthcare and wellness
